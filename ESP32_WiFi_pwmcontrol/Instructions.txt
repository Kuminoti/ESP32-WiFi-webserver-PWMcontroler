Enter your WiFi name und your password in the code and upload.
The serial monitor will Output the ESP's IP-Adress.
Paste the IP-Adress inside your webbrowser and a HTML-document will show up.

"Aktivate" your pins with clicking on the onbutton and Control your PWM-divices
on the pinns 0, 2 and 4 (More pinns will be added soon) with the HTML-sliders.
The HTML-document ist stored inside the "wabpage" variable its easy to modify
if you know some HTML, CSS and Javascript. 
I used an AJAX method to create the PWM-signals.
